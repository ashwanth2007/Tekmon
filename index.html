Tekmon Data Import Hub

A centralised workspace for uploading lead and company lists, mapping the required fields, and syncing structured data directly into your Airtable workflow.

About

This portal supports Tekmon's outreach operations by allowing teams to upload custom contact and company lists and integrate them directly into the Airtable database.

It handles CSV and XLSX parsing, required field mapping, optional column forwarding, and structured webhook dispatch. All from a single clean interface.
